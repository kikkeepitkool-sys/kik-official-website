KIK Sports Cooling Spray is engineered for active lifestyles. Powered by the right amount of menthol, the fast-evaporating formula creates an immediate cooling sensation when sprayed onto clothing. Compact and easy to carry, KIK Sports is the ideal companion for training sessions, competitions and everyday lifestyle.

Designed for athletes, gym-goers and outdoor enthusiasts, this fabric-application cooling mist helps you stay fresh and comfortable during intense activity. KIK Sports is your everyday sideKIK. (puns intended)

DIRECTION - Shake well before use. Spray onto clothes from a distance of 10 to 15 cm. Use whenever cooling is needed.

WARNING - This product can only apply on clothes. Do not use it directly to the skin. When spraying with your clothes on, avoid direct contact with skin. Not suitable for individuals sensitive to alcohol vapors. Test on a small area of fabric before use. Keep out of reach of children. Discontinue use if irritation occurs. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep away from heat, sparks, and open flame.
